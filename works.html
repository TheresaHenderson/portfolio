Theresa Henderson

Currently employed by the Squaxin Island Tribe, I am involved in various communications projects, including creation of web sites, using mostly Wordpress. I publish a daily rss feed from the tribe's main site with approximatley 400 subscribers and a nearly 50% open rate. In addition to this, I design and produce annual reports, museum advertisements, and spcial projects, such as a booklet describing the rare "wet site" archaeological site on the shores of Eld Inlet.
