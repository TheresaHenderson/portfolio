About

Employed as the Public Information Officer for the Squaxin Island Tribe, I have the unique opportunity to participate in numerous and varied tribal communications projects. One of the most interesting was promotion of the Paddle to Squaxin, Canoe Journey 2012. Approximately 30,000 people attended from all across the Pacific Northwest. Communications activities included development of a website and numerous printed materials, including maps, and travel guides, etc.
